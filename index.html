WHY / 01

After badly crimped cables, misconfigured repeaters, and enough questionable home-network fixes, guessing was no longer good enough. Ping Tester exists to turn “the internet feels wrong” into evidence.

Different failures. One timeline.

Find where the connection
stops behaving.

Test the router, a public DNS server, or any hostname together. Separate target histories help tell a local fault from an upstream outage.

03

Provider or internet

Local targets answer while external destinations drop out.
